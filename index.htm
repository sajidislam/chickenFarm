Deliciousness in every bite!

Every cookie made at the ChickenFarm Cookie company is crafted with only the best organic ingredients. Butter from grass fed cows. Unbleached unbromated all purpose flour with the best flavor and textures. Organic sugar only from India. Eggs from chickens that listen to classical music by Mozart while foraging on our pastures. Even our water is special. It comes from the Cascade Mountain springs that we hike up to retrieve. Baking is our passion and we take it very seriously.
